[h1]Features[/h1]

Still activating Distributing Luxury Goods every ten year? This mod adds two indefinite planetary decisions:
[list]
    [*]Permanently Encourage Pop Growth (Bringing it back)
    [*]Permanently Distribute Luxury Goods
[/list]

And can help you
[list]
    [*]Automatically activate the decisions on newly conquered planets or on new colonies
    [*]Activate the decisions on all planets with one click
    [*]Sum the cost of the decisions
[/list]

[h1]Details[/h1]

The decision costs 0.83 food or consumer goods every 10 pops per monthï¼Œless than 10 pops' part also counts. Each planet costs 8.3 at most. [spoiler]max(10, ceil(num_pops/10))*0.83[/spoiler] This cost is updated annually.

Only when you have 20 monthly food income and free housing on the planet the food decision will be auto activated. Similarly, you need 20 consumer good income to auto activate the goods decision.

[b]ATTENTION, DECISIONS WON'T BE AUTOMATICALLY CANCELLED WHEN YOU ARE IN DEFICIT. YOU NEED TO MANNUALY CANCEL THEM.[/b]

[h1]Usage[/h1]

In planetary decisions and Edicts->Auto & Permanent Planetary Decisions: Settings.
